Upholding Academic Excellence and Moral Standards

Why Enfant Cheri?

In Enfant Cheri, your child is assured of the following:

  • Fluency in oral and written English communication;
  • Warm family atmosphere;
  • Formation of strong morals and values;
  • Academic excellence balanced with the development of other types of intelligences;
  • Uncompromised high standards;
  • Curriculum which keeps up with the global education trends;
  • Maintained ideal ratio of students to teachers (25:1);
  • Real, healthy and constant interaction among parents, teachers and students.

History

They shared the vision with a retired Public Schools District Supervisor, Mrs. Nila C. Quieta, who helped them turn it into reality. A corporation was born out of a commitment forged by five incorporators whose primary objective is to provide quality education to the children of Butuan City and ultimately, to the whole region of Caraga...
