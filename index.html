Why Enfant Cheri?

In Enfant Cheri, your child is assured of the following:

  • Fluency in oral and written English communication;
  • Warm family atmosphere;
  • Formation of strong morals and values;
  • Academic excellence balanced with the development of other types of intelligences;
  • Uncompromised high standards;
  • Curriculum which keeps up with the global education trends;
  • Maintained ideal ratio of students to teachers (25:1);
  • Real, healthy and constant interaction among parents, teachers and students.

About Us

  • History
  • Mission/Vision

The idea of putting up a school was conceived by three hardworking teachers: Rose Tampil, Flora Aurea and Nelly Peña. They shared the vision with a retired Public Schools District Supervisor, Mrs. Nila C. Quieta, who helped them turn it into reality. A corporation was born out of a commitment forged by five incorporators whose primary objective is to provide quality education to the children of Butuan City and ultimately, to the whole region of Caraga.

The school started its operation in 1992 with 88 Nursery, Kindergarten 1 and 2 pupils in Mrs. Quieta’s residence. The number of enrollees grew steadily year after year, which pushed the administrators to expand. A more spacious lot in Purok 1A, Upper Doongan, Butuan City was purchased in 1996.

The construction of a new school building started in School Year 2003-2004 which has been its permanent home since. Because of its new location, two of the Board of Directors, Nila C. Quieta, and her son, Denilo C. Quieta, voluntarily withdrew their membership from the corporation leaving the three incorporators determined to go on with their endeavors.

The school has climbed to excellence in the past years. It has shaped winners in various inter-school contests in the Divisional, Regional, and National Levels. Each pupil carries with them the marks of a true Cherian – God-fearing, respectful, well-mannered, brilliant, and fluent in speaking in English.

In School Year 2010 – 2011, the school branched out to San Francisco, Agusan del Sur. The satellite school bears the same mission-vision, philosophy of education, curriculum, programs, rules and regulations as the mother school. It started with 78 Nursery and Kindergarten pupils and three teachers.

Presently, Enfant Cheri continues to soar to greater heights. Adapting to the “new normal”, it currently offers Modified Home Schooling to its students where they will continue to learn through online classes with trained and equipped teachers who will teach, and with the cooperation of the students’ parents who will provide guidance.

Mission

Enfant Cheri is committed to the youth’s holistic development in pursuit of academic excellence complemented by a kind and firm discipline exercised within the context of academic freedom and school-parent partnership. It seeks to strengthen the learners’ belief in a Supreme Being regardless of religious denominations, race and ethnicity.

Vision

Enfant Cheri Study Centre, Inc. envisions the Cherians to be effective communicators, critical and creative thinkers and globally competitive individuals who are prepared for the next phase of their life’s journey, the tertiary education or the world of work.

Milestones

  • 1991 – The school was conceived in October.
  • 1992
    • Registered with Securities and Exchange Commission on June 9
    • School opened on June 16
    • Granted permit to operate preschool on July 2
  • 1994 – Granted Government Recognition for Preschool on Sept. 23
  • 1996 – A 2,500 sq m lot in Barangay Upper Doongan was bought
  • 1999 – Granted Government Recognition for the Elementary Course on July 29
  • 2002 – Construction of the first story of the three-story building started on Nov. 8
  • 2005 – Granted Permit to operate High School on March 16
  • 2007
    • Construction of the 2nd story started on January 7
    • Speech laboratory was installed in May
    • Science Laboratory was completed in July
    • Granted Government Recognition for the Secondary Level in December
  • 2010 – The three-storey school building in Butuan City was completed in April
  • 2012 – A 2,850 square meter lot in Brgy. Alegria, San Francisco, Agusan del Sur was bought
  • 2013 – The construction of a two-storey building in Brgy. Alegria started in January
  • 2014 – ECSCI- San Francisco Branch was granted Government Recognition for Preschool in September
  • 2015
    • ECSCI- San Francisco Branch was granted Government Recognition for the Elementary Course in July
    • The school (main campus) was certified as a FAPE (Fund for Assistance to Private Education) member school which gives fifty (50) Grade 7 students the privilege to receive tuition subsidy from the government
    • The second story of the school building in Brgy. Alegria was constructed for the opening of its Grade 7 level
  • 2016 – ECSCI, SFADS was granted a permit to operate Grades 7 – 9
  • 2018 – The 2,251 sq m lot adjacent to ECSCI main campus was purchased in April for expansion
  • 2019 – Another lot with an area of 1,700 sq m in Brgy. Ampayon near Camella Prima was purchased for expansion
