Milestones

1991
- The school was conceived in October.

1992
- Registered with Securities and Exchange Commission on June 9
- School opened on June 16
- Granted permit to operate preschool on July 2

1994
- Granted Government Recognition for Preschool on Sept. 23

1996
- A 2,500 sq m lot in Barangay Upper Doongan was bought

1999
- Granted Government Recognition for the Elementary Course on July 29

2002
- Construction of the first story of the three-story building started on Nov. 8

2005
- Granted Permit to operate High School on March 16

2007
- Construction of the 2nd story started on January 7
- Speech laboratory was installed in May
- Science Laboratory was completed in July
- Granted Government Recognition for the Secondary Level in December

2010
- The three-storey school building in Butuan City was completed in April

2012
- A 2,850 square meter lot in Brgy. Alegria, San Francisco, Agusan del Sur was bought

2013
- The construction of a two-storey building in Brgy. Alegria started in January

2014
- ECSCI- San Francisco Branch was granted Government Recognition for Preschool in September

2015
- ECSCI- San Francisco Branch was granted Government Recognition for the Elementary Course in July
- The school (main campus) was certified as a FAPE (Fund for Assistance to Private Education) member school, which gives fifty (50) Grade 7 students the privilege to receive tuition subsidy from the government
- The second story of the school building in Brgy. Alegria was constructed for the opening of its Grade 7 level

2016
- ECSCI, SFADS was granted a permit to operate Grades 7 – 9

2018
- The 2,251 sq m lot adjacent to ECSCI main campus was purchased in April for expansion

2019
- Another lot with an area of 1,700 sq m in Brgy. Ampayon near Camella Prima was purchased for expansion
